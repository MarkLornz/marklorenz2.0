I'm a first-year IT student who's still finding his footing in the world of programming. Right now, I know the basic fundamentals of C++ and a little bit of HTML — nothing complex yet, but enough to feel the spark of what's possible. Just like how every great film starts with a single frame, every great program starts with a single line. I'm learning, experimenting, and slowly building a bridge between my love for storytelling and the logic of technology. Creativity and code aren't that different — both begin with an idea, and both take patience to bring to life.
